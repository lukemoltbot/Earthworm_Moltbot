# Australian Lithology Research - Source Bibliography (42 References)

## Government Agencies & Geological Surveys (15 Sources)

3. **Geoscientific Datasets and Reports** - Publicly available geophysical and geological datasets from national mapping programs.

## Data Quality Assessment

### Primary Data Sources (Tier 1)
- **Geoscience Australia databases** - Highest quality, nationally consistent
- **State geological surveys** - Regionally specific, well-documented
- **CSIRO research** - Scientifically rigorous, peer-reviewed

### Secondary Data Sources (Tier 2)
- **Academic research papers** - Peer-reviewed, methodologically sound
- **Industry technical reports** - Practical applications, variable quality
