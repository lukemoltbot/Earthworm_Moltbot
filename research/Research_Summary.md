### 3. **Key Findings**
- **Coal**: Very low density (1.2-1.8 g/cc), low gamma ray (10-30 API), high resistivity (100-2000+ ohm-m)
- **Shale**: Moderate density (2.4-2.8 g/cc), high gamma ray (80-140 API), low resistivity (5-20 ohm-m)
- **Sandstone**: Variable depending on cleanliness and fluid content
- **Carbonates**: High density, low gamma ray, high resistivity when tight
